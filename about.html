Our Story

Founded in 2005, Solids and Stripes Pool Table services has been dedicated to bringing high-quality pool tables to enthusiasts in Sri Lanka. Our journey began with a passion for the game and a commitment to craftsmanship. Over the years, we have grown from a small local business to a respected name in the industry, known for our exceptional products and outstanding customer service.

At Solids and Stripes Pool Table Services, we take pride in offering high-quality, custom-made pool tables that combine elegance, durability, and exceptional craftsmanship.
