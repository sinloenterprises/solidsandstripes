Queens Model Pool Table


The Queens Model Pool Table stands out among other pool tables due to its superior craftsmanship, beautiful design, and exceptional playing characteristics.

• It is constructed with a sturdy wood frame, which provides enhanced durability and stability that will last for years to come.
• While most tables come with basic felt covers, the Queens Model features a professional-grade felt surface designed for smooth and fast ball rolls, ensuring unmatched accuracy and control for players of all skill levels.
• Unlike pool tables with basic pockets, the Queens Model incorporates drop leather pockets, adding a luxurious and traditional charm.
• Additionally, this model features solid wood rails and intricately detailed legs, providing a modern aesthetic that will elevate the decor of any room.
• Unlike cheaper models that may rely on lower-quality cushioning, the Queens Model utilizes the highest-grade rubber cushioning, providing consistent bounce and responsiveness, giving enthusiast an advantage in accuracy and shot execution.

Available in a variety of finishes to suit a variety of tastes, the Queens Model offers a unique blend of style, durability, and performance that sets it apart from standard or entry-level pool tables.
