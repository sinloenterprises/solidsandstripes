Our Premium Services

Installation

Professional installation services to ensure your pool table is set up perfectly for optimal performance and longevity. Our experienced technicians carefully assemble and level your table, guaranteeing precise alignment and stability. We use specialized tools and techniques to ensure every component, from the slate to the cushions, is perfectly positioned. Trust us to deliver a seamless installation experience, so you can enjoy your new pool table with confidence and ease.

Maintenance

Regular maintenance services to keep your pool table in top condition. Inspect and tighten the rail bolts periodically to maintain cushion responsiveness. Level the table by adjusting the leg levelers to prevent uneven play. Additionally, protect the table from direct sunlight and spills to preserve the quality of the wood and felt. With these simple steps, your pool table will remain in excellent condition for years of enjoyment.

Repair

Expert repair services for any issues with your pool table. When your pool table needs repair, our skilled technicians are here to restore it to its original condition. We handle all types of repairs, including felt replacement, cushion reattachment, and slate leveling. Our team uses high-quality materials and precise methods to ensure your table plays like new. Whether it's a minor fix or a major overhaul, we are dedicated to providing top-notch repair services that extend the life of your pool table.
